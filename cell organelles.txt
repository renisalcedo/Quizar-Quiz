What is the "brain" of the cell? Nucleus
What is the "powerhouse" of the cell? Mitochondria
Which organelle stores food, water, and waste in the cell? Vacuole
What does photosynthesis take place in? Chloroplast
What turns amino acids into proteins? Ribosome
What transports materials throughout the cell? Endoplasmic Reticulum
What breaks down food molecules and digests old cell parts? Lysosome
What controls what comes in and out of the cell? Cell membrane
What manufactures ribosomes? Nucleolus
What type of cells has a cell wall? Plant cell
